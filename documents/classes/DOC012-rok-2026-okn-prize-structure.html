Important Scoring Rules

  • Non-Finishers: A competitor who does not finish the race (DNF) receives 5 points less than the lowest-placed finisher
  • Definition of a Finisher: To be classified as a finisher, a driver must complete at least 67% of the race distance (rounded down to the nearest whole number of laps)
  • Dropped Heats (National): In the National Championship (which consists of 6 heats per weekend), a competitor is allowed to drop their single worst heat result from that combined weekend. However, a heat cannot be dropped if the driver was excluded/disqualified from it or did not start
  • Tie-Breakers: If points are tied at the end of the season/event, the winner is decided by the greatest number of 1st places. If still tied, it moves to 2nd places, then 3rd places, and so on. If a tie remains, the highest score in the last race takes preference
Prize Eligibility & Terms

MSA4 Tyre Package: The R100,000 MSA4 tyre prize is awarded to the OK-N National Champion and must be used for the 2027 MSA4 Championship season. The prize is administered through approved MSA4 tyre suppliers and covers race tyres only (not practice or testing tyres). The package is non-transferable and must be claimed within the 2027 season.

Season Champion: The OK-N National Champion is determined by total championship points across all 8 rounds. This prize package is specifically designed to support the critical transition from top-level karting into professional single-seater racing.

General Terms: All prizes subject to full compliance with technical and sporting regulations throughout the season. Drivers under penalty or suspension at season end may forfeit prizes at the discretion of MSA and ROK Cup South Africa. International prize entries are subject to the respective championship's eligibility requirements.
