What the 2026 format is designed to do
More racing mileage
Double-header weekends increase meaningful seat time and accelerate driver development through volume, pressure, and repetition.
Pool engines and parity
Centrally prepared engines are rotated across competitors to keep performance inside a tight band and keep the focus on race craft.
A competitor can drop one heat on a weekend.
